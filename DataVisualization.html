Video Game Sales Visualizations

In the following website are presented several visualizations on Video Game Sales and Ratings Data set (https://www.kaggle.com/kendallgillies/video-game-sales-and-ratings) This data set contains a list of video games with sales greater than 100,000 copies along with critic and user ratings.

Top 10 Games
This visualization shows the top 10 games based on global sales or user/critic ratings for a specified time period (data from 1976 to 2016).
Game sales over time
This visualization shows the game sales through years. The bars are stacked so it can be easy to visualize global sales and the user can also view the data for a single region (Europe, North America, Japan or Rest of the World). It is also possible to view the sales as a percentage for a given year, making it easier to identify the region with most sales for that year.
Platform and Publisher Sales through time
At last, in this visualization are presented the evolution of the platforms or publisheres with most video game sales (top 4 when possible) through the years, like an interactive video
